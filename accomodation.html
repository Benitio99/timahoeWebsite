Accomodation

Timahoe has no accomodation to speak of in the villege itself but there are a number of very nice option quite close by.

Midlands Park Hotel

Arguably Portlaoises nicest Hotel, the Midlands Park Hotel, is now under new management and caters to all the high calibre visitors to the area.

With 116 rooms, three restaurants, a spa, a gym and spa onsite, we're sure The Midlands Park will have something to suit your needs.
